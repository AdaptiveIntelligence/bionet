Fourier Transform analysis of C. elegans undulation muscle activation.
Requires the FFTW3 package: http://www.fftw.org
Usage: undulationFFT 
	[-body_joints <number of body joints>]
	[-motor_sequence_length <length of motor sequence>]
	[-unsynch_joint_activations (unsynchronize joint activations)]
	[-fixed_joint_activations (fixed joint activations)]
	[-random_activations (randomize all activations)]
	[-random_seed <random seed>]
